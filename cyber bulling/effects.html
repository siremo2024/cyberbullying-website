The Effects of Cyberbullying on Learners

Home What is Cyberbullying Effects on Learners How to Stop Cyberbullying

Emotional and Psychological Impact

Cyberbullying can lead to feelings of depression, anxiety, and low self-esteem. Learners who are victims of cyberbullying may feel isolated, ashamed, and powerless.

Academic Impact

Learners facing cyberbullying may experience a decline in academic performance. The stress and emotional toll may make it difficult for them to concentrate in school or attend regularly.

Mental Health Issues

In extreme cases, prolonged cyberbullying can contribute to mental health issues such as anxiety disorders or even suicidal thoughts. It is important to address these concerns early.
